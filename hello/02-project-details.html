Project:


Investment Platform

  • TEAM:
  • Product Owner
  • 2 SMEs
  • Agile Project Manager
  • 3 designers
  • Front-end Team
  • Back-end team

1

USER RESEARCH & REQUIREMENTS GATHERING

Regular interdisciplinary meetings with team members from each department (subject matter experts from client side, who represented the user base, one front-end developer, one back-end developer, one designer) to document the requirements in JIRA.

We have also organised a user field research, when I went to join one of our subject matter experts to see how she is doing her daily work, asked questions and took notes.

2

WIREFRAMES

Designers created wireframes with Balsamiq based on the requirements. Analysts reviewed these wireframes and provided feedback. Iterations until the analysts felt that the wireframes meet the requirements.

3

UI DESIGN

The design team created UI mockups based on the wireframes and simultanously started to put together a component library to keep consistency and help to speed up the front-end development.

4

USABILITY TESTING

While being part of the design team, helping document the requirements, working on wireframes, UI mockups, clicakble prototypes and the UI library, I took the initiative to prepare a usability testing session with end-users through a clickable Invision prototype.
